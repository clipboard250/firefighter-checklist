🔥 Firefighter Application Checklist

Your step-by-step roadmap to becoming a certified Florida firefighter.

✅ Start Here

  • 📋 Meet the requirements: Be 18+, no felonies, non-smoker, in good shape. Details
  • 🚒 Apply to the SPC Combined Firefighter/EMT Program
  • 💪 Register for and pass the PAT test: Call 727-302-6873 to schedule
  • 📝 Apply to SPC (college): spcollege.edu/apply

📎 Documents You'll Need

  • 💉 Physical exam + immunization records
  • 🩺 CPR (Healthcare Provider) certification
  • 🧠 TABE test scores (10th grade level)
  • 🔍 Background check + drug screen

🎯 After You're Accepted

  • 📚 Complete the Firefighter I & II courses + EMT training
  • 🧪 Pass state certification exams
  • 🔥 Become eligible to work as a Florida firefighter!
